Anti-matter atoms in the depths of space are different to the atoms in the world. Instead of having electrons swirling around a nucleus made from protons and neutrons, anti-matter atoms have anti-electrons swirling around a nucleus made from anti-protons and anti-neutrons. Anti-electrons and anti-protons and anti-neutrons also belong to the Particle Zoo. These and other anti-particles can occur inside anti-matter atoms, or by themselves.

When matter bumps into anti-matter something amazing happens. The matter and antimatter annihilate! Annihilation means that something is destroyed and disappears. This annihilation creates an explosion which makes photons to appear.
