"The story of Gilgamesh is one of the oldest recorded stories, anywhere on earth. The story is well over 4,000 years old and we have clay tablets from 3,000 years ago that tell the story.

Gilgamesh was a Babylonian King, who sought eternal life, according to the story. Gilgamesh met strange and terrifying creatures, powerful goddesses, and wise elders. He fought terrible monsters like Humbaba, together with his friend Enkidu.
