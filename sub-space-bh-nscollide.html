Neutron Stars Collide

 

When two neutron stars collide, incredible things happen.

The new object weighs much more than the Sun but cannot support its own weight. In a fraction of a second, it collapses to form a new black hole.

This creation of a new black hole can be very violent, producing jets of particles and bursts of gamma rays. These gamma ray bursts can be seen by satellites orbiting the Earth.

The creation of black holes in this way sends ripples throughout space. These ripples pass by the Earth every day!
