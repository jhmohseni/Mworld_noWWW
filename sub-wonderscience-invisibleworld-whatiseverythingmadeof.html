What is Everything Made Of?

Everything you see around you is made from very tiny things called atoms. You can make anything you see in the world by putting atoms together. They're so small that millions of atoms sitting side by side would be as thick as a hair on your head!

You can't see an atom sitting by itself it's invisible! Yet when you put enough atoms together, you can make anything you see in the world around you.
