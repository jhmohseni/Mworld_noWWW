Using an electron microscope is a bit like giving your eyes a super power the power to see something previously invisible atoms!

When you look at atoms in an electron microscope, they appear as fuzzy white dots arranged in various patterns. It took a long time for scientists to find clever ways to build an electron microscope able to see atoms. They can take pictures of these atoms for you to see, making the invisible world visible!
